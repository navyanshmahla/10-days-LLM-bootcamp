Prompt Engineering

Venture into the realm of prompt engineering, the strategic technique behind optimizing interactions with Large Language Models (LLMs). This module unravels the science and art of crafting precise context to garner desired outcomes from LLMs. By diving deep into the principles of in-context learning and the nuances of prompt design, you'll gain insights into the intricate dance between human queries and machine-generated responses.
As you journey through this section, you'll discover the foundational concepts that underpin effective communication with LLMs.
